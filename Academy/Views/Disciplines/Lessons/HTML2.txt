<h2>HTML Documents</h2> <br>
All HTML documents must start with a document type declaration: [[<]]!DOCTYPE html[[>]]
<br><br>
The HTML document itself begins with [[<]]html[[>]] and ends with [[<]]/html[[>]].
<br><br>
The visible part of the HTML document is between [[<]]body[[>]] and [[<]]/body[[>]].
<br><br>
[[<]]!DOCTYPE html[[>]]<br>
[[<]]html[[>]]<br>
[[<]]body[[>]]<br>
<br><br>
[[<]]h1>My First Heading</h1[[>]]<br>
[[<]]p>My first paragraph.</p[[>]]<br>
<br><br>
[[<]]/body[[>]]<br>
[[<]]/html[[>]]<br>
<br><br>
<h2>HTML Headings</h2><br>
HTML headings are defined with the [[<]]h1[[>]] to [[<]]h6[[>]] tags.<br><br>

[[<]]h1[[>]] defines the most important heading. [[<]]h6[[>]] defines the least important heading:<br><br> 

Example<br>
<h1>This is heading 1</h1><br>
<h2>This is heading 2</h2><br>
<h3>This is heading 3</h3><br>
